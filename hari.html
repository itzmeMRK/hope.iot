What You Will Learn - Week 3

Continue your IoT journey with automation projects. This week, you'll build a smart plant watering system, work with IoT weather stations, and refine your troubleshooting skills.

Day 1: Smart Plant Watering System
Day 2: IoT Weather Station
Day 3: Troubleshooting & Debugging

  • Day 1 - Smart Plant Watering System

    Build an automated plant watering system using a soil moisture sensor and relay module. Learn how to detect soil dryness, trigger a water pump, and create an efficient irrigation system.

    2 Hours per Session 3 Weeks Hands-On Project
    Join Beta Camp

  • Day 2 - IoT Weather Station

    Create a weather monitoring station using an ESP8266 Wi-Fi module and DHT11 sensor. Learn how to send temperature and humidity data to the cloud and visualize real-time weather conditions on ThingSpeak.

    2 Hours per Session 3 Weeks Cloud Integration
    Join Beta Camp

  • Day 3 - Troubleshooting & Debugging

    Learn essential debugging techniques for hardware and software issues. Understand how to identify faulty connections, fix common coding errors, and optimize circuit performance.

    2 Hours per Session 3 Weeks Interactive Session
    Join Beta Camp

What You Will Learn - Week 4

In the final week of the IoT Beta Camp, you will brainstorm and develop a custom IoT project, work on its coding and circuit design, and present your project for feedback and certification.

Day 1: Final Project Brainstorming
Day 2: Project Development
Day 3: Project Showcase & Feedback

  • Day 1 - Final Project Brainstorming

    Collaborate with peers and mentors to brainstorm ideas for your custom IoT project. Define your project goal, list the required components, and plan the overall circuit and coding structure.

    2 Hours per Session 4 Weeks Creative Planning
    Join Beta Camp

  • Day 2 - Project Development

    Begin building your IoT project. Work on coding, assembling circuits, and testing sensor integrations. Apply troubleshooting techniques to optimize your project for functionality and efficiency.

    2 Hours per Session 4 Weeks Hands-On Implementation
    Join Beta Camp

  • Day 3 - Project Showcase & Feedback

    Present your final project to mentors and fellow participants. Receive feedback, refine your design, and earn certification based on project execution, creativity, and presentation.

    2 Hours per Session 4 Weeks Final Evaluation & Certification
    Join Beta Camp

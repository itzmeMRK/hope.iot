OUR COURSES

What You Will Learn

Explore our 4-week IoT Beta Camp designed to give you hands-on experience in IoT technology. From basic Arduino setup to building real-world projects, our curriculum covers it all.

Week 1: IoT & Arduino Basics
Week 2: Smart Light & Temperature Monitor
Week 3: Plant Watering & Weather Station
Week 4: Final Project Showcase

  • Week 1: IoT & Arduino Basics

    Kick off your journey by exploring essential IoT concepts and the fundamentals of Arduino. Learn to set up the Arduino IDE, understand core components like sensors and breadboards, and build a simple LED blinking circuit during our hands-on lab sessions.

    2 Hours per Session 1 Week Practical Introduction
    Join Beta Camp

  • Week 2: Smart Light & Temperature Monitor

    Build advanced projects by integrating Bluetooth modules and sensors. Learn to code a smart light that responds to mobile app inputs, monitor temperature and humidity using the DHT11 sensor, and explore cloud integration via ThingSpeak for real-time data tracking.

    2 Hours per Session 1 Week Project-Based Learning
    Join Beta Camp

  • Week 3: Plant Watering & Weather Station

    Advance your skills by creating automated systems. Connect soil moisture sensors and relay modules to build a smart plant watering system, and integrate an ESP8266 Wi-Fi module to develop a weather station that displays real-time environmental data.

    2 Hours per Session 1 Week Interactive Labs
    Join Beta Camp

  • Week 4: Final Project Showcase

    Consolidate your learning by developing a custom IoT solution. Brainstorm innovative ideas, build your project prototype, and present your work in a final showcase session where you'll receive expert feedback.

    2 Hours per Session 1 Week Project Certification
    Join Beta Camp
